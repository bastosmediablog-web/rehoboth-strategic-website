Meet Our Founders

Jacob & Justina Oladipupo

Founders of Rehoboth Strategic LLC

Rehoboth Strategic LLC was founded by Jacob and Justina Oladipupo, a husband-and-wife team with a shared passion for sustainable agriculture, healthy living, and excellent food production.

Their work is focused on providing fresh leafy vegetables, quality seeds, and healthy seedlings for families, home gardens, and farms. They believe agriculture can strengthen communities and create lasting abundance.

Through integrity, innovation, and dedication, Rehoboth Strategic LLC continues to grow as a trusted source of agricultural products and farming solutions.
