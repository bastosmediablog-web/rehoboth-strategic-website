Meet Our Founders

Jacob & Justina Oladipupo

Founders & CEOs, Rehoboth Strategic LLC

Rehoboth Strategic LLC was founded by Jacob and Justina Oladipupo, a husband-and-wife team driven by a shared passion for sustainable agriculture, healthy living, and excellence in food production.

Together, they are committed to cultivating premium organic leafy vegetables using environmentally responsible farming practices. Their vision is to provide fresh, nutritious produce that supports healthier families and stronger communities.

Through integrity, innovation, and dedication, they continue to build a trusted agricultural company that delivers quality products while promoting sustainable farming.

Our Mission

To provide fresh, organic vegetables that promote healthy living while supporting sustainable agriculture and excellent customer service.

Our Vision

To become a leading supplier of premium organic agricultural products recognized for quality, innovation, and integrity.
